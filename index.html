Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Vishful Attendance ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you use our employee attendance management application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information to provide attendance management services:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, and employee ID.
  • Attendance Data: Check-in and check-out times, work hours, and leave records.
  • Biometric / Face Data: Facial images and face templates used for attendance verification (explained in detail in Section 2).
  • Device Information: Device type and operating system for app functionality.

2. Biometric and Face Data

What we collect: When an employee registers and marks attendance, the app captures a facial image and converts it into a mathematical face template. This template is used solely to verify the employee's identity during check-in and check-out.

How we use it: Face data is used exclusively for employee attendance verification. It confirms that the person marking attendance is the correct registered employee. It is not used for any other purpose, including advertising, tracking, or profiling.

Sharing and storage: Face data is NOT shared with any third parties. It is stored securely on our protected servers with encryption and is accessible only to the employer's authorized account.

Retention: Face data is retained only while the employee is actively associated with the organization. When an employee is removed, or when the account is deleted, the face data is permanently and irreversibly deleted from our systems.

Consent: Face data is collected only with the employee's explicit consent at the time of registration.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to: verify employee identity for attendance, track work hours and attendance records, process leave requests, generate payroll-related attendance reports, and improve app functionality.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal or biometric data with third parties for marketing purposes. Data may only be accessed by your employer's authorized administrators within your organization.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption to protect your data, especially sensitive biometric information, against unauthorized access.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal and biometric data. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

7. Children's Privacy

This app is intended for use by employees and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
